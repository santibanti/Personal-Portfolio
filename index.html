About Me

I'm a Computer Science and Mathematics student at the University of San Diego with a passion for building systems that are both rigorous and useful. My work spans full-stack web development, machine learning research, and network infrastructure — I like problems that sit at the intersection of theory and real-world engineering.

Most recently I interned at Flo Networks as a Software Engineering Intern, where I built a data pipeline connecting FastAPI, a large language model, ClickHouse, and Streamlit to visualize live network topology. Before that I worked as an ML Researcher at USD, improving anomaly detection for drone power logs using an LSTM autoencoder, achieving a 75% detection rate, a 30% improvement over the prior model.

Outside of class and work I enjoy keeping up with developments such as agentic AI, as well as other recreational activities such as traveling and sport.
